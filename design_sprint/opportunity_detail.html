Government Contract: AI-Driven Data Analytics Platform

Client: Department of Advanced Technology Deadline: September 30, 2025 Estimated Value: $5M - $10M Location: Remote / Washington D.C.

The Department of Advanced Technology is seeking a highly qualified vendor to design, develop, and implement a secure, scalable, and AI-driven data analytics platform. This platform will be critical for processing vast amounts of government data to derive actionable intelligence, improve operational efficiency, and support policy-making. The project emphasizes robust cybersecurity measures, integration with existing legacy systems, and the deployment of advanced machine learning models for predictive analytics and anomaly detection.

Key Requirements:

  • Expertise in secure cloud architecture (AWS GovCloud, Azure Government)
  • Proven experience with large-scale data ingestion and processing pipelines (e.g., Apache Kafka, Spark)
  • Demonstrated capability in developing and deploying advanced AI/ML models (e.g., Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
  • Strong background in government contracting and compliance (e.g., FedRAMP, NIST)
  • Experience with legacy system integration and API development
  • Ability to provide ongoing maintenance and support
