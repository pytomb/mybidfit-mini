Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my company profile?

Navigate to 'My Profile' from the main navigation. You can edit your company details, capabilities, and upload past projects there.

What are AI Credits and how are they used?

AI Credits are used for advanced AI-powered features like the Research Sandbox. You can monitor your usage and purchase more in the 'Usage & Billing' section.

How does mybidfit match me with opportunities?

Our AI analyzes your profile, capabilities, and preferences against opportunity requirements. We provide a match score and multi-perspective feedback to help you understand the fit.

Can I collaborate with other suppliers on mybidfit?

Yes, mybidfit facilitates strategic partnerships. You can discover complementary businesses and use our collaboration workspace for joint proposals.
